# Prayers for Work Protection and Blessing

## A Prayer for Work Protection through St. Michael the Archangel
Heavenly Father, I come before You this morning, grateful for the gift of my work and the opportunity to use my talents. I humbly ask for Your divine protection over my endeavors.

Through the powerful intercession of St. Michael the Archangel, I pray:
- Guard my workplace from all harm, discord, and negativity.
- Protect my colleagues and me from ill will, envy, and all spiritual attacks.
- Defend my tasks and projects from errors, setbacks, and delays caused by malice or distraction.
- Inspire me with wisdom, clarity, and strength to fulfill my duties diligently and with integrity.
- Drive away any evil influence that seeks to undermine my efforts or create division.

May St. Michael, the defender of God's people, stand vigilant beside me, guiding my steps and shielding me from all that would hinder my ability to serve You and others through my work.

I offer this prayer with faith and trust, in the Name of Jesus Christ, our Lord. Amen.

## Prayer to Bless Your Work
"Lord Jesus, blessed are You for this work that You provided me, and for the gifts and the skills that you placed in me to help me accomplish my duties. 

Teach me to do my very best every day, to always please You and bring satisfaction to everyone. Bless each and every one of my coworkers: may Your divine presence shine through me, enlighten and comfort them. 

Make me an instrument of Your peace where there are conflicts. Make my heart truthful where there are lies. Make my words just where there is malevolence and gossip.

Make me a source of joy where there is sadness. 

May I always seek You in everything, O my Lord.

Amen."

## Prayer to St. Joseph, Patron of Workers by Pope Pius XII
"O Glorious St. Joseph, model of all those who are devoted to labor, obtain for me the grace to work conscientiously, putting the call of duty above my natural inclinations, to work with gratitude and joy, in a spirit of penance for the remission of my sins, considering it an honor to employ and develop by means of labor the gifts received from God, to work with order, peace, moderation and patience, without ever shrinking from weariness and difficulties, to work above all with purity of intention and detachment from self, having always death before my eyes and the account that I must render of time lost, of talents wasted, of good omitted, of vain complacency in success, so fatal to the work of God. All for Jesus, all through Mary, all after thine example, O Patriarch, St. Joseph. Such shall be my watch-word in life and in death.

Amen."
